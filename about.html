LET ME INTRODUCE MYSELF

I’m a Full Stack Software Engineer who began my journey at Thurstan College, Colombo 07. Over time, my curiosity for technology evolved into a passion for building complete digital solutions. I hold a professional certification from ICET, which has strengthened my foundation in software development. Currently, I’m pursuing my BSc (Hons) in Information Systems at Colombo University, where I continue to expand my knowledge in both front-end and back-end technologies. I'm driven by a desire to create user-focused, scalable applications that solve real-world problems.

I specialize in developing full-stack applications using technologies like JavaScript, Node.js, React, Express, MongoDB, MySQL, and Java. Whether it’s building responsive front-end interfaces or designing robust back-end logic, I enjoy turning complex requirements into clean, efficient, and scalable applications. Beyond coding, I love exploring new tools, staying up to date with tech trends, and continuously pushing myself to grow as a developer. My goal is to contribute to impactful projects, collaborate with creative minds, and build software that truly makes a difference.
